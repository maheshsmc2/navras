Saiyaara arrives as one of the most anticipated romantic films of 2025, and largely delivers on its promise — though not without stumbling along the way.

Ahaan Panday is the real discovery here. Raw, magnetic, and surprisingly restrained for a debut, he commands the screen in a way few first-timers manage. The music, predictably for a Mohit Suri film, is the backbone — and it works.

Where the film struggles is in its second half, which drags considerably and leans on familiar romantic drama tropes without subverting them. The screenplay needed one more draft.

If you love the genre and have a soft spot for big emotional moments and great songs, Saiyaara is worth your time on OTT. For the big screen, it's a maybe.
